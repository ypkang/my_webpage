About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Engineering (CSE) department at the University of Michigan. Advised by Jason Mars, Lingjia Tang and Trevor Mudge and affiliated with the Computer Engineering Lab (CELab). My research interests are in system design and intelligent applications.
